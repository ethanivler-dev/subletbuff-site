How SubletBuff Works

The easiest way for CU Boulder students to find and post subleases. Whether you're looking for a place or listing yours, here's everything you need to know.

Finding Your Sublease

1

Browse Listings

Head to the Listings page and explore available subleases. Use filters to narrow by price, neighborhood, number of beds/baths, distance from campus, and lease dates.

2

Check the Details

Click any listing to see the full detail page — photos, description, lease dates, security deposit, distance from campus, and more. Every listing is manually reviewed before it goes live.

3

Contact the Lister

Use the "Text Lister" or "Email Lister" button right on the listing page. You can also copy their contact info. Reach out, ask questions, and schedule a tour if you'd like.

4

Move In

Once you and the lister agree on terms, coordinate the move-in details directly. Many listings offer flexible move-in dates — look for the "Flexible Move-In" tag.

Posting Your Sublease

1

Sign In with Google

Click "Sign In" in the top navigation and use any Google account. Your email is used for contact so renters can reach you directly.

2

Fill Out the Listing Form

Go to Post a Listing and fill in the details — address, rent, lease dates, photos, description, and contact info. The form auto-saves your progress so you won't lose your work.

3

Submit for Review

Once you submit, a SubletBuff admin will review your listing to make sure everything looks good. This usually takes less than 24 hours.

4

Go Live & Get Contacted

After approval, your listing goes live on the site. Interested renters will reach out via your preferred contact method. You can track views from your Account dashboard.

Why SubletBuff?

Boulder-Only

Built exclusively for CU Boulder students. Every listing is in the Boulder area, with distance from campus on every card.

Manually Reviewed

Every listing is reviewed by a real person before it goes live. No spam, no scams — just real subleases from real students.

Totally Free

No fees to browse or post. SubletBuff is a free tool for the CU Boulder community.

Photos & Details

Listings include photos, descriptions, lease info, and contact details — everything you need to make a decision.

Direct Contact

Text or email the lister directly from the listing page. No middleman, no waiting — connect instantly.

Lister Dashboard

Track how many views your listings get from your Account page. See what's working and manage your posts.
