Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how SubletBuff collects, uses, and protects information when you use the platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account and contact data (for example: email, profile identifiers).
  • Listing data (for example: address, pricing, photos, availability, description).
  • Operational data (for example: timestamps, basic usage and moderation signals).
  • Communications you send to support or through report forms.

2. How We Use Information

  • To operate, secure, and improve SubletBuff.
  • To publish and manage listings and user interactions.
  • To prevent abuse, investigate reports, and enforce platform rules.
  • To send service and transactional communications.

3. Legal Bases and Processing

We process information for legitimate interests in running a housing marketplace, to perform requested services, and to comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share data with infrastructure providers and service partners needed to run the platform, and when required by law or to protect platform safety.

5. Data Retention

We retain data as long as needed for operational, legal, and safety purposes, including abuse prevention and recordkeeping.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no system is completely secure.

7. Your Choices

  • You may request updates or deletion of certain account/listing information, subject to legal and safety requirements.
  • You can contact us to ask privacy questions or submit requests.

8. Third-Party Services

SubletBuff uses third-party tools for hosting, authentication, storage, and maps/geocoding. Their processing is governed by their own terms and privacy notices.

9. Children

SubletBuff is not intended for children under 13.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: support@subletbuff.com.
