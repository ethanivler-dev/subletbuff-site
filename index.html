Stay safe while subletting

Meet in a public place

Always tour the unit and meet the current tenant in person before signing anything.

Never wire money

Use traceable payment methods. Don’t send deposits via Zelle or Venmo to strangers.

Get it in writing

Make sure you have a signed sublease agreement before moving in or paying a deposit.

SubletBuff is a listing platform — we help connect renters and listers but do not manage leases or payments. Always do your own due diligence.
