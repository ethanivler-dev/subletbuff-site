Built for Safe Subletting

Meet in a public place

Always tour the unit in person before paying anything. Bring a friend or meet the lister in a public spot first. Never sign a sublease sight-unseen.

Never wire money

Use traceable payment methods only. Never send deposits via Zelle, Venmo, or wire transfer before touring and signing a written sublease.

Get it in writing

Use a written sublease agreement that includes dates, rent amount, deposit terms, and both parties' names. Confirm that the original landlord allows subletting.
